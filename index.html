Who We Are

One-Stop Readymade Garments Solution

BNS Asia Sourcing offers complete apparel solutions from our Dhaka headquarters — with foreign hub workstations around the globe. We research future-compatible markets and ensure uninterrupted relationships between our clients and origin office.

Our creative design team continuously develops seasonal collections — Spring/Summer presented end of April, and Autumn/Winter end of October. With our own sampling room, we develop fresh queries in just 3–4 days for local yarns and 10–12 days for foreign yarns.

Our Operations

Core Strengths

A complete in-house ecosystem — from design and sampling through QA and technical support — built around our core promise: zero tolerance on quality.

✏️

Own Designing Team

Our creative team from Ibiza presents bi-annual development collections — Spring/Summer in April and Autumn/Winter in October — translating global trends into production-ready tech-packs.

🧵

Independent Sample Section

We have our own sampling room. Fresh queries are developed in 3–4 days for local yarns and 10–12 days for foreign yarns, with full transparency at every stage.

⚙️

Technical Support

Our textile graduate engineers guide factories from yarn selection through fabric finishing — providing daily troubleshooting to achieve perfect fabric and garment fit.

🔬

In-House Lab

Our detailed in-house lab cross-checks fabric and garments daily. Inline QA randomly picks items from the production floor to verify quality before goods are released.

✅

Zero Tolerance QA

Our own QA system monitors every process end-to-end. In-house final inspection of packed goods is carried out before buyer QA teams conduct their own checks.

🌍

Global Presence

With foreign hub workstations around the globe, we research future markets and maintain uninterrupted relationships between our clients and the origin office.

How We Work

From Brief to Shipment

Step 01

Brand Brief & RFQ

We receive your product requirements, target retail price, quality standards, and volume forecasts.

Step 02

Design & Development

Our Ibiza-based design team creates tech-packs, sourcing options, and seasonal proposals within days.

Step 03

Sample Production

Samples produced in our own room — 3–4 days for local yarn, 10–12 days for foreign yarn — shipped for approval.

Step 04

Bulk Manufacturing

Approved designs enter production with daily in-house lab checks and inline QA at every stage.

Step 05

Final QC & Shipment

In-house final inspection of packed goods, then buyer QA sign-off and coordinated on-time delivery.
